Ranked Beginner Tips

1. Use all 15 seconds [bronze-gold]
Never select brawler impulsively. When you find good brawler, tap him, but don't select him. Try to find better pick. I've noticed many people (even me) at lower ranks just double tap brawler and don't select right build (gears, gadgets, etc.) so remember to not double click and select right build.

2. Focus on banning new brawlers.
New brawlers are often broken and hard to play against with.

3. Know when to make advantage of modifiers.
Sometimes it is better to pick other brawler ,even though it doesn't make advantage with modifiers, because it could for example add needed diversity for the teamor counter enemy picks.

4. Learn the meta
In my opinion the best way to learn it is to watch Youtube videos about it. I recommend CryingMan and SpenLC

5. Upgrade atleast one class of every brawler to power 11.
You will have advantage. I recommend upgrading brawlers that you play a lot or from meta.
