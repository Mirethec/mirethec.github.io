Ranked Beginner Tips 1. Use all 15 seconds
Never select brawler impulsively. When you find good brawler, tap him, but don't select him. Try to find better pick. I've noticed many people (even me) at lower ranks just double tap brawler and don't select right build (gears, gadgets, etc.) so remember to not double click and select right build. 2.
